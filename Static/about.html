About Blahgua

blahgua is a self-sustaining content sharing network that respects user privacy and does not require constant tending to achieve relevant results.

Verified but not Identified™: The badging service (in profile) bridges the gap between fully identified and fully anonymous, enabling communication from verified members of companies or industries.

Privacy: Users control how much the system knows about them, with no personal information or email address required for use.

Dynamic distribution: Content is automatically distributed based on the creator's reputation and the interest from the network. There are no friends, followers or direct exchanges.

Feedback loop: Users can see how their posts and comments are performing in the network with in-depth statistics and charts.

Personalized content: Streams are populated based on signals from viewers, not personal information. This allows users to passively watch or actively comment, promote and share.

*Optimized for Chrome (Win/Mac), IE10, Safari and iPhone (pin to desktop)
